# 사랑이 다가올 때 — 웹툰 스토리보드

> BPM:  | 장르: This hyper-Kpop fusion bursts with high-tempo industrial pop, opening with metallic synth stabs and distorted bass layered over Daeguem glitch motifs and rapid Kkwaenggwari percussion. The verses feature sharp falsetto and stutter-gated, granular vocal chops, weaving a futuristic cyberpunk texture anchored in Korean traditional scales. Sudden binaural rewind FX create spatial twists, while glitch elements and relentless beats keep energy peaking, delivering an experimental, high-impact sound.
